The Differentiated Proposition
Full-Funnel Commerce Attribution:
Online + Offline
Logan's Metamobility tracks a user's physical movements and purchase behaviour in the real world. Criteo tracks that same user's online shopping behaviour, cart activity, and transactions. Together, we can connect a digital ad impression to an in-store purchase — closing the attribution gap that most advertisers in LATAM still cannot solve.

A CPG brand like Nestlé or Coca-Cola can run a programmatic campaign through Logan Ads, retarget high-intent shoppers via Criteo's commerce engine, and then measure whether those users actually walked into a store and bought the product — using Logan's real-world location data as the attribution layer. That is a proposition no other partnership in LATAM can currently offer.
